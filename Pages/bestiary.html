Bestiary

The isle of Spearhead is more than snow, stone, and pine. Its wilderness is alive with creatures both natural and otherworldly, shaped by ancient magic and the unseen hand of the Fey. Some stalk the forests in silence, swift and cruel; others crawl from the depths of sea and cavern, alien in form and purpose. Whispers tell of twisted beings once banished from the mainland, of predators clad in living armor, and of Fey-born tricksters whose speed and malice defy mortal reckoning. Whether born of cold earth, deep waters, or the shadows of the Feywild, these creatures mark the island as a place of peril where every step beyond the firelight carries risk.
