Characters

Leonnin

Tall and broad-shouldered, Leonnin carries himself with the steady confidence of one who has sworn his life to a cause. His black hair frames sharp hazel eyes, and his presence commands respect even in silence. He dresses in dark purple, the color of devotion and defiance, with a single glove marked by the triskelion—symbol of Trithereon, god of liberty and retribution. A cleric of freedom, Leonnin believes no chain is unbreakable and no tyranny unchallenged. His faith fuels both his healing touch and his wrath in battle, making him a steadfast protector of his companions and a relentless foe to those who would oppress others.

Raven Silvarion

Raven is a figure of quiet intensity, her black hair swept to one side, the other shaved close to the scalp. Dressed head to toe in fitted black leather, she moves like a shadow between shadows. Two curved black daggers rarely leave her hands, their edges glinting only in the moment before a strike. Her demeanor is sharp, her silence calculated. Where others charge or hesitate, Raven slips unseen, striking with precision and vanishing just as swiftly. In a land filled with danger, she embodies survival through cunning, speed, and skill.

Cate Windrunner

Graceful and keen-eyed, Cate carries the aura of the wild with her wherever she goes. Her long golden hair shines like pale sunlight, and her features are marked by an otherworldly beauty that speaks of her elven heritage. Clad in simple yet elegant traveling garb, she bears a finely crafted bow, the weapon never far from her side. Cate’s bond with the natural world is unshakable. She reads the forest as one might read a map, every sound and sign speaking to her. In battle, her arrows fly swift and true, striking from the treeline with deadly precision. To her companions, she is both guide and guardian—an unerring pathfinder in the vast, snowbound wilds.
