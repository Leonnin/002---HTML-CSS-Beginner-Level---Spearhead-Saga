Session 1 - The Cold Awakening

Shipwrecked on the western shore of the isle, Cate, Leonnin, and Raven awoke in the freezing surf with little more than their wits and scattered supplies. Struggling against hunger, fog, and bitter cold, they made camp and forged a fragile alliance. Their first night brought danger from the forest’s edge—amphibious, frog-like creatures wielding spears. Though wounded, the companions fought them off, marking both their first victory and the beginning of their shared journey.
