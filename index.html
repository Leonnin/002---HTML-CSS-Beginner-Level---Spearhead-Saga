Spearhead Saga

Nestled against the frozen coast of a distant isle, the town of Spearhead Landing endures the bite of winter and the shadows of the pinewood that surrounds it. Snow-laden streets and crackling hearths offer fleeting warmth, but beyond the timber walls, something stirs. Amid this encroaching darkness, three unlikely companions find themselves bound together by fate: Leonnin, a cleric of Trithereon who seeks freedom and justice; Raven Silvarion, a rogue who walks the shadows with quiet defiance; and Cate Windrunner, a ranger attuned to the wild places of the world. Their tale begins here, in snow and silence, where the first sparks of the Spearhead Saga are struck.

Locations

The journey of the party begins in an unknown island after a ship crash, where fate first drew Leonnin, Raven, and Cate together. Their first steps into adventure revealed that the isle holds more than snow and silence - it hides lurking dangers and whispers of greater threats yet to come.
